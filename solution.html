The solution

Raiku introduces a coordination layer on top of Solana.

Instead of submitting transactions and hoping they land, execution can be planned and scheduled ahead of time.

This shifts Solana usage from probabilistic execution to predictable execution.

Raiku does not replace Solana. It coordinates how Solana’s blockspace is used.
